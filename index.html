Approach

GARBAGE IN, GARBAGE OUT

Today's Generative AI systems are only as good as the data they are trained on and have access to. We are fanatical about data quality and security, applying modern data engineering practices to curate trustworthy data.

CONTINUOUS EVERYTHING

Just like with modern software, AI systems are never complete. We are true believers and practicioners of continuous everything... improvement, testing, integration, delivery, deployment, and ATO.

HUMANS IN THE LOOP

AI is a product of human creativity and intelligence. We believe humans are the most important component of the multi-agent systems we build and provide for intelligent interfaces between man and machine.

Iron Majors

Defense General Intelligence built Iron Majors to showcase the power of multi-agent AI frameworks in military applications.

We've built a system that enables human operators to organize multiple, domain specific AI agents into a cohesive team to accomplish complex tasks.

AI agents are trained for a specific problem set and have access to tools and data that are relevant to their mission.

Human agents can be injected at any point in a process, shaping the AI agents' output and providing context and guidance.
